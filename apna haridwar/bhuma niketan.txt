Shri Bhuma Niketan is situated on Saptsarovar Marg. Bhuma Niketan is an Ashram in which a temple is also situated. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and Vishnu. At the entrance of the temple a large statue of Lord Shiva holding the charred body of Shiva is situated. In side the temple there is a mechanized jhanki (tableau) of many Hindu mythological events. There is also an artificial cave inside the temple. Figurines of many Hindu God and Goddesses is also there in the temple. The Ashram also provides stay option.
